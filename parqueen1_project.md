### Parqueen
Meghan Stang, Jenn Lao, Harper Bellows, Norm Schultz 

### Pitch
Parqueen alerts you of the legality of where you park their car and notifies you before your parking has expired.

### Problem
Drivers get parking tickets because of not seeing or understanding parking signs or remembering to move their car.

### Solution
Alert you of the legality of where you park and when your parking will expire.

### Target Audience
Anyone with a smart phone who parks a car in an urban environment.

### Integrations
APIs: Google Maps, COORD Curb Search
OAuth: Google for user login
